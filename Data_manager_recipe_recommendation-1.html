Ingredients

Spaghetti Pasta - 1/2 kg (or desired portion per package)
Bacon/Pancetta - 200g
Eggs - 3 large
Parmesan Cheese - 1 cup (freshly grated)
Black Pepper - To taste (freshly ground)
Salt - To taste

Instructions

Step 1
Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
Cook pasta according to package directions
Reserve 1 cup of pasta cooking water before draining
Step 2
While pasta cooks, cut bacon into small pieces
Cook in a large pan until crispy (about 5 minutes)
Remove from heat but keep the bacon fat in the pan
Step 3
In a bowl, whisk together eggs, cheese, and pepper
Add cooked pasta to the pan with bacon
Quickly stir in the egg mixture while the pasta is hot
Step 4
Add pasta water as needed for desired consistency
Season with salt and additional pepper to taste
Serve immediately with extra grated cheese
