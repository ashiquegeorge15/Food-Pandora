Privacy Policy

Welcome to Food Pandora! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Food Pandora platform, located at foodpandora.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account or interact with our platform, we may collect personal details like your name, email, and other information you provide.
  • Usage Data: We collect information on how you use our platform, such as the recipes you search for, the pages you visit, and other behavioral data.
  • Third-Party Integrations: If you connect with third-party services (e.g., fitness apps), we may collect data from those services based on their privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our platform's features, including personalized recommendations and accurate nutritional data.
  • To communicate with you about your account, updates, and promotional offers (you can opt-out at any time).
  • To ensure the security of our platform and prevent fraud.
  • To comply with legal requirements and protect the rights of our users.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party providers who assist us in operating and improving our services.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

4. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications by unsubscribing from emails.
  • Request information about how we use your personal data.

5. Security of Your Information

We use reasonable security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page, and we will indicate the effective date of the changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at support@foodpandora.com.
